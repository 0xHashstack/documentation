# Withdraw the borrowed assets to your wallet

1. Go to “Your Borrow” tab from the dashboard and choose the loan which you want to partially withdraw.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (39).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Choose the “Withdraw Partial Borrow” option from the dropdown in the Borrow Actions tab.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (44).png>)



3. Fill in the amount and click on “Withdraw Partial Borrow” button.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (50).png>)



4. Sign the transaction.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (52).png>)



5. If there are no issues, then it will showcase the success notification. You should receive the tokens in your wallet.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (46).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
